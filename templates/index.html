LEARN ABOUT CTF

Capture The Flag (CTF) is a type of cybersecurity competition where participants solve challenges to find "flags" — specific pieces of text hidden within the tasks. These challenges can range from cryptography, web security, reverse engineering, forensics, and more. CTF events are an exciting way for cybersecurity enthusiasts to test and enhance their skills, learn new techniques, and compete against others in real-world scenarios. CTF competitions are widely used in the cybersecurity community to improve problem-solving skills, teamwork, and hands-on experience. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced security professional, CTF offers a fun and educational environment for everyone.
